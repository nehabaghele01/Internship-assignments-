🧺 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk

📦 Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Cake pan
  • Oven

👩‍🍳 Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add milk and oil; whisk until smooth.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes and enjoy!
